Bridge Ventures targets established Chinese investors to provide partnerships with lucrative Israeli startups on the cutting-edge of bio-medical innovation.

OUR MISSION

At Bridge Ventures, we provide the unique opportunitiy for the affluent Chinese financial market to sponsor the rapid growth of the Israeli bio-medical field. We believe that our firm can bridge cultural barriers and help advance quality and precision of medical care worldwide.

OUR VENTURES 

At Bridge Ventures we survey the sea of cutting-edge bio-medical startups in Israel to identify future leaders researching Interventional Cardiology, Atrial Fibrilation, and Benign Prostatic Hyperpalsia. Our close ties to Chinese investors will allow us provide these startups with the support needed to develop technologies that can save countless lives worldwide.
